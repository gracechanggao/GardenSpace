How to start my GardenSpace?

1.10.2016

Step 1. Get everything you need

We help you choose the right plants for your area and your space, ship them to you with the right soils, nutrients and our SmartGarden box. No know how needed.

Step 2. The fun part

Once your GardenSpace has arrived you get the fun part of planting your amazing garden in your SmartGarden box. Easy, fun and simple.

Step 3. Your SmartGarden handles the rest

Your SmartGarden monitors your growing environment and will send you a notification when growing assistance is needed. Fresh produce management free.
